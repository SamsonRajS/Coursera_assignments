Pizza

Pizza was first invented in Naples, Italy as a fast, affordable, tasty meal for working class Neapolitans on the go. Pizza is a dish consisting of usually round, flat base of leavened wheat- based dough topped with tomatoes,onion,chicken, cheese and often various other ingredients.

Hamburger

A hamburger is a food consisting of one or more cooked patties usually ground meat placed inside a sliced bread roll or bun. The patties maybe pan fried, grilled, smoked or flame broiled. Hamburgers are often served with cheese, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, baconor chilis.

Cake

Cake is a form of sweet food made from flour, sugar and other ingredients that is usually baked. The most commonly used cake ingredients include flour, sugar, eggs, butter and a leavening agent such as baking powder. In their oldest form cakes were modifications of bread and were topped with cream.
